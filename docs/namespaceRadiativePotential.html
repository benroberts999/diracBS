Detailed Description

Ginges-Flambaum QED Radiative Potential.

  • [1] V. V. Flambaum and J. S. M. Ginges, Phys. Rev. A 72, 052115 (2005).
  • Including small finite-nuclear size corrections:
  • [2] J. S. M. Ginges and J. C. Berengut, Phys. Rev. A 93, 052509 (2016).

Functions calculate V_rad(r) for each r, given r_N (nuclear radius) in atomic units, z, and alpha. Note sign: H -> H - V_rad, but H -> H + H_rad.
